  1. Texture

    First and foremost, i really hate the texture of a tomatoe. Tomatoes are never a good texture.

    They always tend to be the worst texturing of all the types of foods out there. And to specify I am talking about raw tomatoes. Raw tomatoes are the worst abominations of tomatoes known to man kind.

    You never know what you are going to get with a tomatoe. It will always be either wayyy too mushy or it will be over ripe and like you are eating an apple.

    The worst offender of this texture abomination has to go towards those heirloom tomatoes.

    Additionally, it always feels like you are biting into skin. Like they feel a little to uncomfortably like skin for me to happily eat. No thank you sir, I would not like to eat a skin tomatoe.

    • Dont get me wrong, this is coming from a vegitarian who has and will continue to eat all types of fruit and vegetables but TOMATOES ARE THE WORST OFFENDERS.



  2. Taste

    Second of all, they taste terrible!

    No offense to anyone who actually enjoys tomatoes (im sorry but you are a masochist), but these abominations... at this point they dont deserve to be called a "food item".... are absolutely disguisting

    Of course you could take everything that I do say with a grain of salt but god damn, are these things so sour? bitter? literally anything but sweet i think??

    They have no distinct flavour that would make me want to come to them over and over and over again. They just arent that good.

    They are like cilantro. Like my very one personal hell of abomination cilantro. They just make me queasy and hate absolutely everything about life if I am given a raw tomatoe.

    If you even think that they may be a semblence of good... you are lying to yourself.



  3. Seeds

    THE SEEDS. OH GOD THE SEEDS. WHY ARE THEY EVERYWHERE ARE THEY CRUNCHY OR NOT WHAT EVEN I CANNT.

    The seeds are one of the more disguisting aspects of tomatoes that I just cant get around. For raw tomatoes especially, the seeds just get on my nerves.

    The seeds arent necessarily as unnoticable as the strawberry seeds, nor could you just take them out like apple or peach seeds.

    The seeds specifically just have this incredible ability to make me want to puke because there are so many of theme and combined witht the terrible textures that I had mentioned at the top...

    That just makes for the worst combo known to mankind. Please. We need to cancel tomatoes like yesterday.
